Mobile App & Stealth Mode

The mobile experience is designed for maximum privacy and camouflage.

The Calculator Disguise

When you open InnerOrbit on mobile, it appears as a fully functional scientific calculator ("CalcX"). This decoy interface protects your privacy from prying eyes.

Multiple Decoy Interfaces

InnerOrbit provides multiple game-based decoys to suit your preference. You can switch between them using the Floating Action Button (FAB) menu in the game interface.

  • Calculator: The default scientific calculator interface.
  • Tic-Tac-Toe: A fully playable Tic-Tac-Toe game against AI.
  • Ludo Master: A classic board game interface.
  • Guess Number: A simple number guessing game.

All decoys are fully functional games/apps to maintain the illusion.

💡 Stealth Entry: The default way to access your chats is to Triple Tap the "AC" button (Calculator) or the game board/display area (Games).

Access Methods

You can customize how you unlock the hidden messaging interface in Settings > Privacy > Stealth & Camouflage.

  • Triple Tap (Default): Tap the configured button (default "AC") 3 times quickly.
  • Triple Tap & Hold: Tap 3 times and hold the last tap for 3 seconds on the footer "CalcX" logo (Emergency/Alternative).
  • Secret Code: Configure a numeric code (e.g., "7331") and press "=" to unlock.
  • Header Lock: Long press the "Calculator" title for 3 seconds.

Emergency Reset

If you forget your access method, you can reset to the default (Triple Tap AC) by tapping the "AC" button 3 times and holding.

Security Features

End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted using AES-256 with device-specific keys. Only you and your recipient can decrypt messages.

Algorithm: AES-256-GCM | Key Derivation: PBKDF2

Perfect Forward Secrecy

Each conversation has unique ephemeral keys that rotate automatically. Old messages cannot be decrypted even if a key is compromised.

Key Rotation: Per conversation | Timestamp-based

🛡️ Device Security

Encryption keys are stored securely on your device using platform-specific secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android).

Storage: Device-local | Protected by OS-level security

✔️ Safety Numbers

Verify the identity of your contacts using safety numbers. Compare 12-character fingerprints to ensure secure conversations.

Format: XXXXX XXXXX XXXXX (Signal-style)

🚫 Screenshot Detection

On supported platforms, receive alerts when someone attempts to screenshot your conversation.

Supported: iOS, Android | Optional: Can be disabled

🔗 Secure Connections

All communications use TLS 1.3 encryption for transport security. Connections are authenticated with SSL certificates.

Protocol: TLS 1.3 | Certificate Pinning: Enabled

How Your Data is Protected

Key Derivation (PBKDF2)

We use PBKDF2 to generate unique encryption keys for every conversation. This process combines your unique device identifiers with random data ("salt") to ensure that keys cannot be guessed or brute-forced.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Messages are locked using AES-256, a military-grade encryption standard. The "GCM" mode ensures that if anyone tries to tamper with a message while it's being sent, the app will reject it immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is my 4-digit User ID and how is it different from my PIN?

Your User ID (e.g., 1234) is your public identifier that you share with others to receive messages. Your PIN (e.g., 123456) is a private 6-digit code that you use to login along with your User ID. Never share your PIN with anyone.

Are my messages really encrypted end-to-end?

Yes! Every message is encrypted with military-grade AES-256 encryption on your device before it's sent. The servers never have access to the plaintext of your messages. Only you and the recipient can decrypt and read the messages.

What happens when I enable disappearing messages?

Once you set a disappearing timer (5 seconds, 1 hour, or 24 hours), any messages sent in that conversation will automatically delete from both sides after the timer expires. Note: Messages deleted from the server are still visible in local storage until refreshed.

Can I recover a deleted account?

Account recovery depends on how you deleted it. If you used email registration, you can recover your account within 30 days. For anonymous accounts, deletion is permanent. Always save your recovery codes in a safe location.

How do safety numbers work?

Safety numbers are 12-character fingerprints unique to each conversation. You can view and verify them with your contact through an out-of-band method (call, video, in person). If the numbers don't match, it could indicate a security issue. This is optional but recommended for highly sensitive communications.

Is InnerOrbit free to use?

Yes! InnerOrbit is completely free to download and use. We don't have ads, and we don't charge for messaging or premium features. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will always be free.

What platforms does InnerOrbit support?

InnerOrbit is available on: iOS (Apple App Store), Android (Google Play Store), Web (https://app.innerorbit.io), Windows (Desktop app), and macOS (Desktop app). All versions sync seamlessly.

Can I use InnerOrbit offline?

Yes! InnerOrbit has offline support. You can compose messages offline, and they'll be sent automatically when your connection is restored. This works on both mobile and web versions.

Troubleshooting

❌ Messages Not Sending

Solution: Check your internet connection. Ensure the app has permission to access the network. Try refreshing the conversation or restarting the app.

❌ Can't Find a Contact

Solution: Make sure you have the correct 4-digit User ID. User IDs are non-sequential and don't follow patterns. Check the exact spelling/numbers with your contact.

❌ Messages Appear Encrypted

Solution: This usually means a key derivation issue. Try: 1) Log out and log back in, 2) Clear app cache, 3) Reinstall the app if issue persists.

❌ Notifications Not Working

Solution: Check app notification settings in your device Settings. Grant InnerOrbit permission to send notifications. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" mode is off.

❌ Sync Issues Across Devices

Solution: Ensure you're logged into the same account on all devices. Check your internet connection on all devices. Sometimes it takes a few seconds to sync.

❌ Forgot My PIN

Solution: Use the "Forgot PIN?" option on the login screen. If you can't recover it, delete the account and create a new one with a new PIN.

❌ App Crashes on Startup

Solution: 1) Force close the app, 2) Clear cache from Settings, 3) Restart your device, 4) Reinstall the app if issue persists.

❌ High Battery Drain

Solution: Disable location services for InnerOrbit if not needed. Reduce notification frequency. Check if background refresh is enabled unnecessarily.
